## About TaskSpan

TaskSpan is a focused time log app for personal work tracking. Start a timer for a task, pause when needed, stop when done, and keep a clean timeline of where your time goes.

### Core Features

- One-tap start, pause, resume, and stop timer flow.
- Manual entry editor for past work sessions.
- Daily timeline with totals per task.
- Weekly summary grouped by project and task.
- Search and filters by date range, task name, and tags.
- CSV export of filtered history for reports and invoicing.

### Who It Is For

- Freelancers who need lightweight invoicing support.
- Solo professionals who want a clear weekly workload view.
- Students tracking study blocks and project effort.
